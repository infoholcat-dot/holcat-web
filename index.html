Who we are

Craftsmanship rooted
in Wageningen

HolCat Renovations is a team of specialists in house remodeling based in Wageningen, serving the city and its surroundings. We believe that maintaining and repairing homes is essential for well-being — not only for the house, but for the people who live in it.

We have expertise in interior renovations of historical Dutch houses, kitchen design and assembly, bathroom renewals, painting, and finishing surfaces. We work with natural materials and traditional wood techniques.

And because the old must also be preserved, we offer exterior and interior repairs and maintenance, including wooden frames and gutters.
